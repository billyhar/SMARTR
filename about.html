So who are we?

We are SMARTR. A company providing a service for users to either hire a self-driving car or to list their own one, making the most of owning one of these vehicles. The aim of this service is to appeal to anyone looking at cheap alternative forms of transport, with no driver required. It could be used with our service and make you money! We have a specific user filtering system that analyses users profiles, making sure they are trustworthy enough to use your vehicle. We also have an inbuilt geo-field system which you as the lender can control to prevent the vehicle from driving to far away. You can also track your vehicle in real-time via GPS on our web interface and IOS app.









So how does it work?

For a hirer, simply complete your online profile and register your credit card to get started. Login and start searching for self-driving cars in your area. Select a vehicle and destination and you car will be on its way.

For a lender, get started by listing your car using our inbuilt listing service. State the location you are in and what hours your car will be operating for. Our inbuilt star system shows every user with a star rating similar to a credit rating. You can choose whether to only let 5 star users, use your car or 4 star etc.
